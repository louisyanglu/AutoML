##### 2.1.3  评价指标

$MAPE=\frac{1}{m}\sum_{i=1}^{n}\frac{|y_i-y_i'|}{y_i}$

$Huber=\begin{cases}
\frac{1}{2}(y-\hat{y})^2  
  & \text{ if } |y-\hat{y}|<=\delta  \\
\delta |y-\hat{y}|-\frac{1}{2}\delta ^2 
  & \text{ if } |y-\hat{y}|<=\delta
\end{cases}$
